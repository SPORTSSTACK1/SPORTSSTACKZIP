How the winner is decided

  1. Exact score after 90 minutes
  2. Correct result (win, lose or draw)
  3. Who wins, if the 90-minute score is a draw
  4. How they win it, extra time or penalties
  5. First goalscorer
  6. Minute of the first goal
  7. Total cards
  8. Earliest entry

The detail: Own goals count as "Own goal", not as the next scorer. Goals and cards in added time count back to the whistle, so 45+2 is 45 and 90+4 is 90. A yellow counts as one card, a straight red as two, and a red shown for a second yellow as one. If the 90-minute score is 0-0 and you have gone for penalties, there are no goals to predict, so we use the first card instead.
